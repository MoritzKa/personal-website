About Me

My name is Moritz. I support Lightning Network founders and businesses in adopting this technology.

Bitcoin is fascinating - but even more is the Lightning Network.

I am interested in projects of all industry verticals applying the Bitcoin Lightning protocol: ones that are free and open source, ones that disrupt existing verticals with the unfair advantage of Lightning and ones that create a new category and have the potential to become category leading businesses.

I am passionate about entrepreneurship and solving problems that matter together with a team. I have financed businesses and sat on boards from seed to Series A; I have run teams, set up processes, and raised funds.

Previously, I worked as an investment manager at Unternehmertum Venture Capital Partners, a venture capital firm that invests in early-stage technology startups. To date, UVC Partners has helped more than 30 companies launch and scale their businesses. I was engaged on both sides of the venture industry: as an investor and startup manager. At Bosch Digital Solutions we worked with decentralized technologies on building blocks for the digital sovereignty of organizations.

In my free time I like to go running, spend time on outdoor activities and eat spicy food. I am always happy to meet people who share similar passion for these topics.
